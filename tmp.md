
This report analyzes the 'Quality at a Reasonable Price' (QARP) factor. The factor is a composite score designed to identify high-quality companies trading at a fair price, following the investment philosophy of Warren Buffet. It is constructed by ranking and combining three underlying components: Profitability (measured by Return on Equity), Financial Health (measured by Leverage), and Value (measured by Earnings Yield).
